Privacy Policy for Farm Fetched

Last updated: March 2026

Farm Fetched (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Farm Fetched Pi Network application.

Information We Collect

  • Pi Network username (via Pi SDK Login)
  • Public Pi user ID (provided by Pi SDK)
  • In-app actions such as buying animals, feeding, selling, etc.

No sensitive personal information is collected.

How We Use Your Information

  • To save your farm progress
  • To enable Pi payments inside the app
  • To improve game features and user experience

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell or share your data with third parties. Data stays within the Farm Fetched system and Pi Network infrastructure.

Security

We use secure hosting and Pi Network authentication. No passwords or wallet keys are stored by us.
